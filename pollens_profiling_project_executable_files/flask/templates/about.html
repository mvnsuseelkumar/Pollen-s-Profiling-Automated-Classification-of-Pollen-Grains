About Pollen Classification

Understanding the science behind pollen analysis and our AI system

What is Pollen Classification?

Pollen classification is the scientific process of identifying and categorizing pollen grains based on their morphological characteristics. Each pollen grain has unique features such as size, shape, surface texture, and aperture patterns that help scientists identify the plant species they came from.

This field is crucial for various applications including:

  • Allergy research and treatment
  • Archaeological studies
  • Climate change research
  • Forensic investigations
  • Agricultural monitoring
